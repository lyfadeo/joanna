## Index exemple ##

,, each line starting with "start" and ending with "end ;" sets a sequence ,,

start, name Lorem, a play 1 un.wav, b stop 1 3000, c play 2 fx.wav, d next, end ;
start, name Ipsum, a next play 1 deux.wav, c next, d prev, end ;

,, do not begin any comment (or follow any comma) with start or end ,,

start, name Dolor sit amet, a play 1 trois.wav, b goto 1, c custom message, e custom egassem, end ;
